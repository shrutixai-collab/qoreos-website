# QoreOS — Your Business, Upgraded at Its Core.

India's first Claude-native AI Operating System for businesses.

QoreOS builds and installs custom AI employees into Indian businesses — so your team stops drowning in repetitive work and starts focusing on actual growth.

---

## What is QoreOS?

Most businesses use AI as a tool — something they open, type into, and close. QoreOS is different.

We map every part of your business, identify where time and money is leaking, and build AI employees that fix those leaks permanently. Custom-built. Trained on your data. Connected to your tools. Running 24/7.

---

## The AI Employee Stack

### Q:Sales
Never lose a lead again. Qualifies every lead the moment they come in, follows up automatically in Hinglish, books calls, and manages your entire pipeline — the way Indian customers actually communicate.

### Q:CA ⭐ India-exclusive
Your AI finance and compliance brain. Tracks GST, flags ITC mismatches, sends advance tax reminders, and answers finance questions instantly — 24/7. Works alongside your CA, never replaces them.

### Q:Compete
Know your competition before they move. Monitors competitors 24/7 — tracking pricing, product launches, marketing moves, and market sentiment.

### Q:Ads
Ad spend that actually works. Creates ad copy, manages campaigns, optimises spend, and generates performance reports across Meta and Google.

## How It Works

**Week 1 — Audit**
We spend the first week inside your business. Mapping workflows, identifying bottlenecks, understanding where time and money is leaking.

**Week 2–3 — Build**
We build your custom AI employees — trained on your data, connected to your tools, tested until they work exactly as they should.

**Week 4 onwards — Deploy + Optimise**
We go live. We monitor. We improve. Your AI employees get smarter the longer they run.
