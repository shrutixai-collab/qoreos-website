Questions we get asked.

Do I need to be technical to use QoreOS?
Not at all. We handle every part of the build and setup. You just tell us how your business works and what problems you need solved.
How is QoreOS different from just using ChatGPT?
Tools require you to do the work. QoreOS builds systems that do the work for you — running in the background, connected to your actual business tools, producing real outputs without you having to prompt anything.
What tools does QoreOS integrate with?
Gmail, WhatsApp, Google Sheets, Notion, popular Indian CRMs, Tally, Zoho, and most tools your business already uses. We confirm integrations during the audit.
Is Q:CA a replacement for my CA?
No. Q:CA is a finance and compliance assistant that works alongside your CA. It gives you instant clarity, flags risks, and prepares information — your CA still makes the final professional decisions.
How long does it take to go live?
Most clients are live within 30 days of starting. Audit takes one week. Building takes two weeks. Deployment and testing takes one week.
What if I'm not happy with the results?
We don't disappear after deployment. We monitor, fix, and improve every system we build. If something isn't working, we fix it.
